My name is Asiem Bah, a professional tile installer with nearly a decade of hands-on experience in residential and commercial tiling. I trained as an engineering technician, where I mastered tile installation, leveling, waterproofing, and finishing techniques.

Today, I work here in The Gambia, helping homeowners and businesses transform their spaces with clean, durable, and beautiful tile designs. My focus is simple: quality work, honest service, and professional results.
